# ChoreTracker — How-To Guide

A friendly walkthrough for the parent. No tech needed — if you can use a phone, you can run this.

**Your app:** https://chore-tracker-kohl.vercel.app

---

## 1. Add it to your phone (optional but nice)
Open the link in your phone's browser, then:
- **iPhone (Safari):** tap the Share button → **Add to Home Screen**
- **Android (Chrome):** tap the **⋮** menu → **Add to Home screen**

Now it opens like a real app, full screen. Have each kid do the same on their device.

---

## 2. Sign in
Tap **Sign in**, enter your email and password. You land on your **Family** dashboard.

Each child signs in on *their own* device with *their own* email and password (the ones set up for them). They'll only ever see their own chores and points.

---

## 3. Set up chores
Go to the **Chores** tab → **New**.

- **Chore** — what it is ("Take the trash out")
- **Who** — which child it's for
- **Points** — what it's worth
- **Repeats:**
  - **Every day** — shows up daily
  - **Weekly** — pick which days (e.g. trash on Mon + Thu)
  - **One time** — a single day only
- **Active** — turn a chore off without deleting it

Tap the **pencil** to edit, the **trash** to delete.

---

## 4. Set up rewards
Go to the **Rewards** tab → **New**. Give it a name and a **point cost** (e.g. "30 min screen time — 20 points"). Kids save up points and trade them in.

---

## 5. The daily rhythm
1. **Kids check off** chores they finish. They show as "Waiting for Mom."
2. You get them in the **Approve** tab. Tap **✓** to approve (points are added) or **✗** to reject.
3. When a kid redeems a reward, it shows up in **Approve** too — approve to give it, or deny to give the points back.

Everything updates **live** — when a kid checks something off, it appears for you right away, and when you approve, their points update on their screen instantly.

---

## 6. Your dashboard
The **Family** tab shows each kid's points, how many of today's chores are done, and a banner when things are waiting for you.

---

## Good to know
- **Points are only added when you approve** — kids can't give themselves points.
- A chore checked off today is automatically fresh again tomorrow — nothing to reset.
- Forgot a password? It can be reset for any account — just ask.

Questions or want something changed? Just reach out.
